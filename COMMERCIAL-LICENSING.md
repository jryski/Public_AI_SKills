# Commercial Licensing

The skills in this repository are publicly available for individual personal use under `LICENSE.md`.

Use by or for an organization requires a separate written license.

## Who needs a license

A license is required for use by or for:

- a company;
- an employer;
- a client;
- a team;
- a nonprofit;
- a school;
- a government body;
- an institution;
- a commercial project;
- an enterprise, team, or organizational AI workspace.

Individual accounts used for professional or organizational work still require a license.
